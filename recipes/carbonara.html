Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti, a widely used ingredient in many cookings, can be mixed with a large variety of components to provide a great dish. Today were gonna be talking about Spaghetti Carbonara, which is a personal favorite, and how to make it in your own kitchen. To begin, this dish consists of spaghetti (obviously), eggs, bacon, cheese, and black pepper. The history behind this dish is relatively short as it made its first appearance between the 1950s-60s, while being shrouded in mystery and uncertainy. The mystery behind this dish is of the many stories behind it, such as it coming from coal miners, a La Carbonara restaurant from 1912, or during World War 2 where bacon and eggs were a very common thing. Regardless, this recipe is very convenient and simple to make, along with its ability to provide an excellent taste to whomever is consuming. Now that we know a little more about this dish, the next sections will cover the needed ingredients and how to prepare and cook Spaghetti Carbonara.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of Spaghetti
  • Black Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Guanciale, Pancetta, Bacon(budget, not preferred)

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, cheese (shredded preferred), and black pepper in a bowl, then set aside
  2. Boil Pasta until 'al dente', don't rinse pasta
  3. Quickly add the mixture to the hot cooked pasta, add water from pasta boil if needed.
  4. Top with Cheese(shredded preferred)
