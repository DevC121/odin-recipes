Mac & Cheese

Description

Mac and Cheese is another great dish loved by both adults and kids. Macaroni can vary in textures such as it composing of a rich and creamy or with a rough texture (usually less creamier). Mac and Cheese dates as far back as 1769 and is believed to come from Northern Europe. This has spreaded to America thanks to Thomas Jefferson, when after a trip to France, Jefferson was amazed by the pasta dishes there and ended showcasing it once he returned to America. With that, being just a brief explanation of its history, There are plenty of others stories and explanation about its origin. Either way, Macaroni is a great dish to enjoy individually or with others, and for today's ingredients, will be going over how to make this recipe rich and creamy.

Ingredients

  • Elbow Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Milk
  • Half and Half
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
